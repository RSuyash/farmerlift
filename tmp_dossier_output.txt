3. The "QR Code" Manufacturing Strategy 
You identified "Government Rules" as a requirement for QR codes. There are two ways to 
handle this. 
Option A: The "Website Shortcut" (Free) 
We use a free online generator to make a static image and paste it on the page. 
●​ The Danger: This is for display only. If you send this low-quality image to your packaging 
printer, it will blur. If the website link changes, the code on your physical bags becomes 
dead. 
Option B: The "Manufacturing Asset" (Recommended) 
I build a custom "Dealer Download Center" (Admin View). 
●​ The Feature: Your packaging team can log in and download Vector Quality (SVG) QR 
codes for any product. 
●​ The Guarantee: These codes are dynamically linked to our database. They are 
high-resolution for printing on fertilizer sacks and bottles. 
●​ The Value: This saves you from hiring a graphic designer to manually generate 45 
separate codes every season.
